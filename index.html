Who Do We Spend Time With Across Our Lifetime



In a society where everyone has their own busy schedules, struggles, and life events, an examination into the allocation of our time among various aspects of our social existence becomes not only important but critical. The distribution of time among the various individuals we spend time with in our life, such as our family, partner, friends, and even ourselves, reveals important insights into the dynamics of our relationships.

Life is short indeed, and knowing the amount of time we spend with the people in our life, it can help us idetify and adjust the time we spend with them in order for us to maximize our time with the people we love.
